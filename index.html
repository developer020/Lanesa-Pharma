Features

Discover the key features that make Lanesa Limited your trusted partner in healthcare distribution and marketing.

Comprehensive Product Portfolio.

Lanesa offers a diverse range of healthcare products, including pharmaceuticals, surgical equipment, medical equipment, ophthalmic equipment, theatre equipment, and OTC products. We also cater to the veterinary sector, ensuring a wide selection of options for our valued customers.

  • Validated by healthcare professionals adhering to the highest standards.
  • Variety is a cornerstone, we offer a diverse range of healthcare products.

Extensive Distribution Network

With strategically located warehouses and offices in Nairobi, Kisumu, and Mombasa, we provide efficient distribution services throughout Kenya. Our sub-distributors in major towns and trading centers ensure that our products reach every corner of the country.

This expansive network allows us to promptly respond to market demands and maintain our reputation as a trusted healthcare distributor in the region.

Trusted Partnerships

Lanesa Limited has earned the trust of both international pharmaceutical companies and local manufacturers operating in Kenya. We are proud to be their sole distributors, responsible for marketing, distribution, brand promotion, and meeting market requirements.

  • We establish exclusive distribution agreements with international pharmaceutical companies and local manufacturers.
  • We foster collaborative relationships with government procurement agencies, NGOs, and private agents.
  • We provide ongoing marketing support, brand promotion, and tailored solutions.

Personalized Delivery Service

Lanesa offers a personalized delivery service, with our own delivery vans and experienced delivery personnel. For remote customers, we have established partnerships with leading courier and parcel service providers, guaranteeing timely delivery of goods.

With our unwavering focus on personalized service, we aim to exceed your expectations and ensure a seamless healthcare product delivery experience.
